Frequently Asked Questions

01

Who can use ReRover?

Currently, ReRover is available to International University students, administrators, teachers, and staff members. We are actively planning to scale up our services to make them more accessible to a broader community in the future.

02

How do I report a lost item?

To report a lost item, simply click the 'Report Lost Item' button on our homepage. Fill out the form with details about your item, including when and where you last saw it, along with any distinguishing features. You can also upload photos to help identify your item.

03

I found an item. What should I do?

If you've found an item, use our 'Report Found Item' feature to submit the details. Provide accurate information about where and when you found it, and our system will help match it with any existing lost item reports.

04

How does the claiming process work?

When you find a potential match, our system will link the lost and found items and notify both parties. Both users must confirm the match, after which an approval request is sent to administrators for final verification.

05

What happens after claim approval?

If administrators approve the claim, both items are removed from public display and merit points are awarded to both users. If rejected, both items return to public display for potential matches. This system ensures fair and accurate item returns.

06

Is my information secure?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and only used for the purpose of item recovery. We never share your details with third parties without your consent.
